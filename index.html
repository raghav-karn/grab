Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Find answers to common questions about our service, delivery process, and more.

How does Grab work?

Grab connects you directly with local farmers and food producers. Browse our app or website, select the items you want, and schedule a delivery time that works for you. We'll bring fresh local food right to your doorstep!

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to most major metropolitan areas and their surrounding suburbs. You can check if we deliver to your area by entering your zip code on our website or app. We're constantly expanding our delivery zones!

How fresh are your products?

Our products are harvested or made to order and delivered directly to you. This farm-to-table and restaurant-network approach ensures you're getting the freshest possible food, unlike grocery stores where produce may have been in transit or storage for days or weeks, or the $20 40-days-old Erewhon smoothie your pal got you that one day.

Are all farm produce organic?

While many of our farmers follow organic practices, not all are certified organic. Each producer's profile indicates whether they are certified organic or use sustainable farming methods. We prioritize working with farmers who use environmentally friendly practices, regardless of certification status.

What if I'm not home for delivery?

During checkout, you can provide delivery instructions for your Pick-up or Drop-by order. You can also use our Schedule feature. Many customers request that their order be left in a shaded area or with a neighbor. We use insulated packaging to keep perishable items fresh for several hours after delivery.
